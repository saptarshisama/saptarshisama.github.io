About

I’ve always had ideas, but most of them got lost to inaction. This is just a place for me to dump my ideas and half-baked attempts at execution.

About life, I follow my grandfather’s simple yet effective principle, and it goes something like this:

“জীবন যাত্রায়”

“ভোগ থাকবে, লোভ থাকবে না”

“স্নেহ থাকবে, মোহ থাকবে না”

“দীপ্তি থাকবে, দাহ থাকবে না”

“বিকাশ থাকবে, প্রকাশ থাকবে না।”

The rough English translation is this

“In the journey of life:”

“There will be enjoyment, but not greed.”

“There will be affection, but not attachment.”

“There will be radiance, but not burning pain.”

“There will be growth, but not vain display.”
